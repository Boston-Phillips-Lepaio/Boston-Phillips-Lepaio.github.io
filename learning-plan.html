My long term goal/career pathway is to find a job within the field of software and web development. Working from the comfort of my home is a dream of mine.

A strength in learning of mine that can also be seen as a limitation is my desire for perfection. I want everything to be pixel perfect and to be efforlessly efficient. This desire lets me explore deeper than learning competencies require which is great for self-learning but terrible for time management.

I'm a pretty shy keep to myself kinda person. I'd like to break out of my shell and connect more with others here at Dev Academy.

I set a time where I begin work in the morning and end in the evening. Right now I study from 9am to 5pm.

When things build up and start to get to me I talk to my friends and my girlfriend to unwind.

If I've spent more than 15 minutes pondering a single thing and have found myself at a block, I'd ask my facilitator for help

I expect the Facilitation team to be very friendly and helpful.

I don't have any regular scheduled block-out times, my weeks are free to focus on Dev Academy.
