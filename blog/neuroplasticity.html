Understanding neuroplasticity will benefit you people by opening their mind to the limitless potential of their brains. Not only can it help in learning new things easier, it can also help with depression, help recover from brain trauma/injures and even increase your brain volume and memory.

Neuroplasticity can help me achieve whatever I set my mind to, my capabilities for knowledge are boundless as long as I myself believe it so. I'll use these principles to further my goals starting from Dev Academy to a future in tech.

A healthy body equals a healthy mind. To increase your neuroplasticity then you need to first help your body. Eating healthy foods with nutrients that support brain function as well as regular aerobic exercise are some of the few ways to increase neuroplasticity.
